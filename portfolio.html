A Gore-Y Election

A Gore-Y Election was my entry, completed in under 72 hours, for Ludum Dare 31. It features the 2000 election between Al Gore and George H.W. Bush, however the player plays as Al Gore trying to convince players to vote for him.

Math Merchant

One of my first few games, Math Merchant was a simple prototype of a game idea involving players in a post-apocalyptic world where they were the only person intelligent enough to do mathematics. The player would have to construct numbers and give them to customers as answers to their problems. Please note that the game is very compressed, and may take up to a minute to load.
