While developing my programming skill I have also been refining my graphical design skills creating for clients, products for print.

Mostly I am currently working on EterniTech projects but I am very happy to take on any web design work that you may want me to carry out for you. If you would like me to design a website for you, please see the contact us section on the right hand sidebar.
